## 🛡️ Strategic Moat (Financial)
As global trade shifts into digital RoO (Rules of Origin) and self-certification, the **cost of non-compliance** (fines, port delays) becomes the primary risk. Indeutrade mitigates this, serving as **Insurance-as-a-Service** for the 2026 trade roadmap.
